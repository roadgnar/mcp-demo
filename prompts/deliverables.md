# Deliverable Generation Prompts

Turn analysis into stakeholder-ready PDF reports with real street-level photos embedded.

## Reports with Imagery

> Create a PDF report about infrastructure conditions on [street]. Search for street-level imagery of any damage, download the photos, and embed them in the report. Include crash data, pavement scores, and a repair recommendation. Save to results/[topic]/.

> Write a report on the most dangerous streets in Boston. For each street, include a downloaded street-level photo showing conditions. Save to results/dangerous-streets/.

> Generate a sidewalk conditions report for [neighborhood]. Find cracked sidewalks in imagery, download examples, cross-reference with 311 complaints. Save to results/[neighborhood]-sidewalks/.

## Presentations

> Build a presentation comparing infrastructure conditions between two neighborhoods. Include downloaded imagery for each. Save to results/[comparison]/.

> Create a presentation about construction activity in [area]. Include photos of active sites from imagery search. Save to results/[area]-construction/.

## Quick Summaries

> Give me a one-page summary of infrastructure conditions around [location]. Download a few street-level photos to include. Save to results/[location]-summary/.

## Tips

- The best deliverables include real photos — ask Claude to search imagery, download images, then embed them
- Specify a results folder so everything stays organized
- Photos are more persuasive than numbers for non-technical stakeholders
- Ask for recommendations, not just data
