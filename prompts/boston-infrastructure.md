# Boston Infrastructure Prompts

Prompts tailored to Boston neighborhoods, corridors, and datasets. Use with the Boston branch MCP configuration.

## Neighborhood Assessment

> Assess infrastructure conditions in Dorchester -- pavement, sidewalks, and crash history

> What does Roxbury look like from street-level imagery? Show me the worst pavement conditions.

> Compare sidewalk conditions between Back Bay and Mattapan

> Show me infrastructure conditions along Blue Hill Ave from Roxbury to Mattapan

## Safety Analysis

> Where are the most dangerous intersections in Dorchester based on crash data?

> Do pedestrian crashes cluster near schools or transit stops in Roxbury?

> Show me faded crosswalk markings near the top crash locations on Blue Hill Ave

> Are there active work zones near recent crash sites?

## 311 + Imagery Gap Analysis

> Compare 311 pothole complaints in Dorchester with what Cyvl imagery actually shows

> Which neighborhoods have the most sidewalk repair requests, and does imagery confirm the damage?

> Find streets where imagery shows damage but no 311 complaints have been filed

## Equity and Demographics

> How does median household income in Roxbury compare to Back Bay, and how do pavement conditions differ?

> Show me infrastructure quality across Boston's 5 lowest-income neighborhoods using Census data and Cyvl scores

> Do lower-income neighborhoods have more 311 complaints per capita?

## Corridor Deep Dives

> Full infrastructure audit of Blue Hill Ave: pavement, sidewalks, crosswalks, signs, crashes, and 311 complaints

> Compare Washington St conditions in Roxbury vs South End

> Assess Commonwealth Ave from Allston to Back Bay -- what needs attention?

## Report Generation

> Create an infrastructure equity brief for Dorchester and Roxbury with photos, data, and demographic context

> Build a corridor safety report for Blue Hill Ave suitable for a public works director

> Generate a sidewalk accessibility assessment for Jamaica Plain with photo evidence
