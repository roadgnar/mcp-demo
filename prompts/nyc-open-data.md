# NYC Open Data Prompts

Copy-paste prompts for exploring NYC data across all MCP servers. Focus: Queens.

## Queens Infrastructure (Cyvl)

> Search for cracked sidewalks in Jackson Heights, Queens. Show me 3 images with GPS coordinates.

> Find faded crosswalk markings in Long Island City. How many results?

> Show me street signs in Jamaica, Queens. Are any damaged or missing?

> Search for potholes visible in street imagery across all Queens projects

> Find bus stops without shelters in Jackson Heights

## 311 Complaints (Socrata)

> What are the top 10 complaint types in Queens this month? Use NYC 311 data.

> Show me noise complaints near Jackson Heights in the last 7 days

> How do street condition complaints in Queens compare to Manhattan this year?

> What 311 complaints have been filed near 37th Avenue and 74th Street in Jackson Heights?

> Show me the trend in pothole complaints for Queens over the past 12 months, by month

## Restaurant Inspections (Socrata)

> What restaurants in Jackson Heights have a grade C or worse?

> Show me the most common restaurant violations in Queens

> How many restaurants in zip code 11372 were inspected this year? What's the grade distribution?

> Find restaurants in Jackson Heights with critical violations in the last 6 months

## Housing Violations (Socrata)

> What are the most common housing violations in Queens this year?

> Show me open Class C (immediately hazardous) violations in Jackson Heights zip codes

> Compare housing violation rates between Jackson Heights and Jamaica

> How many HPD violations were filed in Queens in the last 30 days? Break down by class (A/B/C).

## Demographics (Data Commons)

> What is the population of New York City? How has it changed over the last 10 years?

> Compare median household income between NYC and the national average

> What is the unemployment rate in New York City?

> Show me housing unit counts for NYC over the past decade

## Cross-Source Analysis

> Compare sidewalk 311 complaints in Jackson Heights with what Cyvl imagery shows for cracked sidewalks in the same area

> Show me the streets with the most 311 complaints in Jamaica, then pull Cyvl imagery for those streets

> What does the data say about infrastructure equity in Queens? Use 311 complaints, housing violations, and demographic data.

> Create a neighborhood profile for Jackson Heights: demographics from Data Commons, complaints from 311, restaurant grades from inspections, and street conditions from Cyvl
